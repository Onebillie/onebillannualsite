How It Works?

Discover how OneBill Utilities makes saving on your utility bills a breeze:

Step 1

Sign Up

Sign up to OneBill Utilities in just a few minutes. All we need from you is some basic information.

Step 2

Choose Your Plan

Whether you have just electricity & gas, or if you have multiple properties to manage, we can help!

View Plans
Step 3

Upload Old Bills

Upload some recent bills to our analyser and we'll see how much you can save. We use your actual usage to check which rates suit you best.

Step 4

Sign OneBill Agreement

Authorise us to manage your bills, request usage and tariff history from suppliers and make any necessary changes on your behalf. We won't switch you unless AutoSwitch is active. We'll bring you the best deals and help you pick.

Step 5

Personalised Portal

We'll bring all of your suppliers bills, usage data, payments and cost information to your personalised OneBill Portal. One Login for all your bills. We currently offer electricity, gas and broadband.

Step 6

Thorough Analysis

We analyse all the info we receive. We'll make sure your bills are correct, that your products suit your needs and that the prices are right, forever!

Step 7

Advanced Budget Plan

We'll calculate a new Budget Plan that covers all of your bills. Our advanced algorithms create a comprehensive Budget Plan, considering seasons, anomalies, past, present & future prices and more.

Step 8

Seamless Payments

We'll set up your payments with any new suppliers and we'll keep a track of your usage to make sure your payments are right.

Let Us Do the Heavy Lifting

Once you're onboard, we take care of the rest - leaving you with more time and more savings.
