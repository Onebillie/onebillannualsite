Smart Meters

2024.07.22

What is a Smart Meter?

What is a Smart Meter? A smart meter is a digital device that records your electricity and gas usage in real-time. It sends this data directly to your energy provider for accurate billing and better energy management.

Benefits of Smart Meters:

  • 1 - Accurate Billing: No more estimated bills. You are charged for the actual energy you use.
  • 2 - Real-Time Monitoring: Track your energy usage in real-time and identify ways to save.
  • 3 - Time-of-Use Tariffs: Take advantage of lower rates during off-peak hours.
  • 4 - Energy Efficiency: Helps you become more energy-efficient by providing detailed insights into your consumption patterns.

How to Know if You Have a Smart Meter:

  • Look for a digital display on your meter.
  • Check for a signal strength indicator or communication features.
  • Review your utility bill or contact your energy provider for confirmation.

How to Use a Smart Meter:

  • 1 - Monitor Your Usage: Use the accompanying in-home display (IHD) to see real-time energy consumption.
  • 2 - Optimize Time-of-Use: Shift energy-intensive activities to off-peak hours to save money.
  • 3 - Review Data: Regularly check your usage data to identify and reduce energy wastage.

Installing a Smart Meter:

  • Contact your energy provider to schedule an installation.
  • A technician will install the smart meter at no extra cost.
