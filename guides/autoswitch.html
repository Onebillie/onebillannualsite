AutoSwitch Explained

2024.07.22

What is AutoSwitch?

AutoSwitch is a service that automatically finds and switches you to the best utility tariffs available, ensuring you always get the best rates without any hassle. It continuously monitors the market and your energy usage to identify savings opportunities.

How AutoSwitch Works:

  • 1 - Sign-Up: Register for the AutoSwitch service through OneBill.
  • 2 - Upload Bills: Provide your current utility bills for analysis.
  • 3 - Market Monitoring: The service continuously monitors available tariffs and deals.
  • 4 - Automatic Switching: When a better deal is found, the service automatically switches you to the new tariff.
  • 5 - Notifications: You receive notifications about the switch and the savings you will achieve.

Benefits of AutoSwitch:

  • 1 - Cost Savings: Always be on the cheapest tariff, saving up to €1200 per year.
  • 2 - Convenience: No need to manually search and switch providers; it’s all done for you.
  • 3 - Unbiased Recommendations: Get the best deals without any provider bias.
  • 4 - Continuous Monitoring: The market is constantly checked to ensure you are never overpaying.
  • 5 - Peace of Mind: Know that your utility bills are being managed efficiently.

Getting Started with AutoSwitch:

  • 1 - Sign Up: Register for OneBill’s AutoSwitch service.
  • 2 - Provide Information: Upload your utility bills and provide any necessary details.
  • 3 - Let OneBill Handle the Rest: The service will monitor, compare, and switch your tariffs automatically.

Why Choose AutoSwitch?

  • Save Time: No more spending hours comparing tariffs.
  • Save Money: Benefit from the lowest available rates.
  • Stay Informed: Receive updates and notifications about your savings.
