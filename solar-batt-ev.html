Solar, Batteries & EVs

2024.07.22

Solar Panels

Solar panels convert sunlight into electricity, providing a renewable energy source for your home. Installing solar panels can significantly reduce your energy bills and your carbon footprint.

Benefits of Solar Panels:

  • 1 - Lower Energy Bills: Generate your own electricity and reduce dependence on the grid.
  • 2 - Environmentally Friendly: Reduce greenhouse gas emissions by using clean energy.
  • 3 - Increase Property Value: Homes with solar panels often have higher resale values.
  • 4 - Government Incentives: Take advantage of tax credits and rebates for solar installation.

Batteries:

Home batteries store excess energy generated by solar panels, allowing you to use it when the sun isn't shining. This maximises the efficiency of your solar power system.

Benefits of Batteries:

  • 1 - Energy Independence: Use stored energy during peak hours or power outages.
  • 2 - Cost Savings: Reduce reliance on the grid and avoid high peak-time charges.
  • 3 - Environmental Impact: Minimise your carbon footprint by using stored renewable energy.
  • 4 - Electric Vehicles (EVs): EVs are vehicles powered by electric motors using energy stored in batteries. Charging your EV with solar power makes your transportation more sustainable and cost-effective.

Benefits of EVs:

  • 1 - Lower Running Costs: Electricity is cheaper than petrol or diesel.
  • 2 - Reduced Emissions: EVs produce zero tailpipe emissions, reducing air pollution.
  • 3 - Government Incentives: Benefit from grants and tax incentives for purchasing EVs.
  • 4 - Quiet and Efficient: EVs are quieter and more efficient than traditional vehicles.

Optimising Solar, Batteries & EVs with OneBill:

  • Smart Tariffs: Use time-of-use tariffs to charge your battery and EV when rates are lowest.
  • Energy Management: Monitor and optimise energy consumption for maximum savings.
  • Earn from Excess Energy: Sell excess solar energy back to the grid for additional income.
